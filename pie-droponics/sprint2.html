Software Updates

The biggest software change from Sprint 1 is that there are now 3 different actuators (water pump, nutrient pump, growing light) that runs on their own respective schedule. While both pumps still run on a simple millis() loop, the light uses actual time (ex. Turn on at 11:50 AM, turn off at 1:00 PM) for scheduling.
