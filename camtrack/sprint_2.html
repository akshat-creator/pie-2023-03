Sprint 2

Video Demo


The goal for this sprint was to get the pan-and-tilt part of our automated tripod working. We 3D-printed the pan-tilt mechanism, which would contain the DSLR camera, and programemd two stepper motors to rotate the camera using this mechanism. Only vertical motion was finalized with this design due to mechanical errors, which would be fixed in later sprints.

Mechanical


The mechanical goal for Sprint 2 was to completely finish CAD for the entire tripod design and begin working on fabrication for the final assembly. Software


The Raspberry Pi ran a Haar Cascade classification program that could detect faces with the attached PiCamera. It calculated the distance from the center of the face from the center of the camera frame and sent that distance to the Arduino over Serial communication to control the servo.
